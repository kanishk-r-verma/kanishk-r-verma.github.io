About Me

Hello ! I'm Kanishk, the Irish Research Council (IRC) Enterprise Scholar, working on developing a Data-driven toolkit to tackle Cyberbullying among teens on social media. I was recently awarded the Online Content Safety Scholarship in 2021 by Google and IRC to pursue my doctoral studies at the School of Computing, Dublin City University, Dublin, Ireland. Previous to this appointment, I worked as a Research Assistant on a project funded by Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Content Policy grant to combat Cyberbullying using Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Social Network Analysis.
